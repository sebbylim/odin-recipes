Lasagna Recipe

Delicious Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomatoes
  • Beef

Steps

  1. Slice everything up.
  2. Mix everything together.
  3. Put it into a pot and then into the oven.
  4. Add a bit of love.
  5. Eat.
